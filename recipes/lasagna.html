Homemade Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce.

How to make Lasagna

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce,tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job. A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles,more sauce, followed by a later of cheese.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!

Ingredients

For the meat sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce)can tomato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • salt

To assemble the lasagna

  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 pounds (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
